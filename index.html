About Our School

Bakka Secondary School is committed to providing quality education that nurtures students' talents and prepares them for future challenges.

We believe in holistic development through academic excellence, character building, and extracurricular engagement. Our experienced faculty and modern facilities create an environment where every student can thrive.

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses to help students achieve their academic goals and prepare for their future careers.

Science Program

Our science program offers comprehensive education in physics, chemistry, and biology with state-of-the-art laboratories.

Arts Program

The arts program encourages creative expression through literature, history, fine arts, and performing arts.

Business Program

Our business program prepares students for careers in entrepreneurship, economics, and commerce.

Our Facilities

We provide modern facilities to support our students' learning and development with state-of-the-art equipment and resources.

Library

Our library has over 20,000 books, digital resources, and quiet study areas for students.

Science Lab

Modern laboratories equipped with the latest technology for physics, chemistry, and biology experiments.
