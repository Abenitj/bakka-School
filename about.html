Our History

Bakka Secondary School was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education to the community.

Founded by a group of passionate educators, our school started with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a small building. Over the years, we have grown significantly while maintaining our commitment to academic excellence and character development.

In 2005, we moved to our current campus, which features state-of-the-art facilities to support our students' learning journey. Today, we serve over 1,200 students with a dedicated team of 80 teachers and staff members.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to our founding principles while adapting to the changing educational landscape to prepare our students for the future.
