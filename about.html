Our History

Bakka Secondary School was founded in 2014 EC with a vision to provide quality education to the community of Bakka, Ethiopia.

Founded by a group of passionate educators, our school began with just 50 students and 9 dedicated teachers in a modest building. Through unwavering commitment to academic excellence and character development, we have grown into a respected educational institution.

Today, we proudly serve over 350+ students with a team of 16+ experienced teachers and staff members, continuing our mission to provide comprehensive education that prepares students for success.

Throughout our journey, we have remained true to our founding principles while continuously adapting to meet the evolving needs of education, preparing our students for success in the 21st century.
