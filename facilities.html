Our Facilities

State-of-the-art learning environment for our students

At Bakka Secondary School, we believe that excellent facilities are essential for providing a comprehensive education. Our campus features modern, well-equipped spaces designed to enhance learning and foster creativity.

Explore Our Facilities

Modern Library

Our spacious library houses over 20,000 books, periodicals, and digital resources. It features quiet study areas, group collaboration spaces, and computer stations for research. The library is open beyond school hours to support our students' learning needs.

  • Extensive collection of reference materials
  • Digital catalog and online resources
  • Quiet reading and study areas
  • Professional librarian support

Science Laboratories

Our fully-equipped science labs provide students with hands-on experience in physics, chemistry, and biology. Designed to meet international safety standards, these spaces foster scientific inquiry and experimentation.

  • Dedicated labs for each science discipline
  • Modern equipment and instruments
  • Safety-first design with emergency equipment
  • Interactive learning technology

Computer Laboratories

Our computer labs feature the latest technology with high-speed internet connectivity. Students learn programming, digital design, and research skills in these modern facilities.

  • High-performance computers
  • Specialized software for programming and design
  • 3D printing and robotics equipment
  • High-speed internet connectivity

Sports Facilities

We believe in the importance of physical education and offer extensive sports facilities including a full-sized soccer field, basketball and volleyball courts, a swimming pool, and indoor gymnasium.

  • Olympic-sized swimming pool
  • Indoor basketball and badminton courts
  • Fully-equipped gymnasium
  • Track and field facilities

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to facilitate interactive learning with comfortable furniture, natural lighting, and technology integration. Each classroom is equipped with smart boards and multimedia capabilities.

  • Interactive whiteboards in every classroom
  • Flexible seating arrangements
  • Natural lighting and climate control
  • Technology integration points

Auditorium & Performing Arts

Our 500-seat auditorium hosts school assemblies, performances, and guest lectures. The performing arts department includes dedicated spaces for music, dance, and drama.

  • 500-seat capacity auditorium
  • Professional sound and lighting systems
  • Music practice rooms
  • Dance studio with mirrored walls

More Facilities

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria serves nutritious meals prepared in a modern kitchen. We accommodate various dietary needs and promote healthy eating habits.

Health Center

The school health center is staffed with a qualified nurse and provides first aid, health screenings, and promotes wellness programs.

Transportation

We provide safe and reliable transportation services with GPS-tracked buses covering all major routes in the area.
