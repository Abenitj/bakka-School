Campus Life

Where Learning Extends Beyond the Classroom

At Bakka Secondary School, we believe that education extends far beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our vibrant campus life offers students countless opportunities to explore interests, develop talents, and build lasting friendships.

Campus Life at Bakka Secondary School

Students compete in various athletic events showcasing teamwork and sportsmanship.

Our talented musicians and performers showcase their skills in our annual concert.

Debate Competition

Students engage in thoughtful discourse on important topics during our annual debate tournament.

Student Activities & Clubs

Performing Arts

Our performing arts programs include choir, band, orchestra, and drama club. Students perform in seasonal concerts and annual theater productions.

STEM Club

The STEM club engages students in robotics, coding, and scientific research projects. Members compete in regional and national science fairs.

Athletics

We offer a wide range of sports including basketball, soccer, volleyball, track and field, and swimming. Our teams compete in interschool tournaments.

Art & Design

The art club allows students to explore various mediums including painting, sculpture, digital art, and photography. Student work is displayed in annual exhibitions.

Community Service

Our community service club organizes volunteer activities, charity drives, and environmental initiatives to help students develop social responsibility.

Debate & Journalism

Students develop critical thinking and communication skills through debate team and school newspaper activities, covering campus events and issues.
